Pork Sinigang

Recipe

A Filipino dish known for its sour taste.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 (1/2 in) piece of ginger (chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 lb of bone-in pork chops
  • 4 cups of water
  • 1 package of tamarind soup base
  • 1/2 lb of fresh green beans

Steps

  1. Heat vegetable oil over medium heat.
  2. Add onion then stir until softened and translucent. Season with salt.
  3. Stir in ginger, tomatoes, and pork chops.
  4. Cover and reduce heat to medium-low. Turn the pork occasionally, until browned.
  5. Pour in water and tamarind soup base. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until pork is tender and cooked through.
  6. Stir in green beans and cook until tender.
