Arroz Caldo

Description

A Filipino soup made with rice and chicken.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion (diced)
  • 2 cloves of garlic, crushed
  • 1 piece of fresh ginger, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 2 chicken wings
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • 5 and 1/4 cups of chicken broth
  • 1 cup of sweet rice
  • salt and pepper
  • 1 green onion, chopped
  • 1 lemon, sliced

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Cook and stir onion, garlic, and ginger in hot oil until fragrant.
  3. Add chiken wings, cook and stir for one minute.
  4. Stir fish sauce, cover and cook for another 2 minutes.
  5. Pour chicken broth into the pot.
  6. Stir in sweet rice and bring mixture to a boil.
  7. Cover and cook for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  8. Season with salt and pepper.
  9. Serve it in a bowl with green onion, lemon, and fish sauce if you want.
