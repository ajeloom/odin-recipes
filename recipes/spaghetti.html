Spaghetti

Description

A famous dish made with pasta and tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 small green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 1 can of tomato sauce
  • 1 can of tomato paste
  • 2 tsp of dried oregano
  • 2 tsp of dried basil
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Combine ground beef, onion, garlic and green pepper in a large pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Cook and stir until meat is browned and vegetables are tender. Drain grease.
  3. Stir diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste into the pan.
  4. Season with oregano, basil, salt, and pepper.
  5. Simmer spaghetti sauce for an hour, stirring occasionally.
